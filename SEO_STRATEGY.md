# AWD Organic Search Strategy

## Search positioning

AWD should build topical authority around a Christian nonprofit organization
that provides faith-based community outreach through men's ministry, women's
ministry, prayer, service, leadership, and giving.

## Priority keyword clusters

| Page | Primary cluster | Supporting terms | Demand |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Home | Christian nonprofit organization | faith-based nonprofit, Christian community outreach | Medium |
| About | Christian nonprofit organization | Christian community service organization, faith and unity ministry | Medium |
| Ministries | Christian men's ministry; Christian women's ministry | men's discipleship, women's discipleship, iron sharpens iron ministry | Niche / high intent |
| Leadership | Christian ministry leadership | servant leadership ministry, ministry directors | Niche / high intent |
| Get Involved | donate to Christian nonprofit | volunteer with Christian nonprofit, faith-based volunteer opportunities | Niche / conversion |

Exact current US volume and keyword difficulty require a paid Semrush, Ahrefs,
Google Keyword Planner, or equivalent account. The current research therefore
uses demand tiers and live search-result intent, and should be refreshed once a
production domain, target geography, and paid keyword dataset are available.

## Content roadmap

Create genuinely useful resources rather than thin keyword pages:

1. What “iron sharpens iron” means for Christian men's ministry
2. How Christian men can build accountability and spiritual leadership
3. Finding identity and purpose through Christian women's ministry
4. Practical ways to volunteer with a Christian nonprofit
5. How faith-based community outreach breaks cultural barriers
6. Prayer resources for families, leaders, and communities

## Launch checklist

1. Confirm the production domain and regenerate absolute sitemap URLs.
2. Verify the domain in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
3. Submit the XML sitemap after deployment.
4. Connect Google Analytics 4 with consent handling.
5. Add the organization's verified address, phone, email, EIN/charity status,
   and social profile URLs to Organization structured data.
6. Create and verify a Google Business Profile if AWD serves a public location
   or service area.
7. Earn relevant links from partner churches, community organizations,
   ministry directories, event partners, and local press.
8. Publish original ministry updates and resources consistently.
